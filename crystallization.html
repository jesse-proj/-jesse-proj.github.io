Crystallization

This separating technique is where the atoms and molecules are formed from a liquid to a solid mixture known as a crystal. This crystal is made out of one atom, assorted species of ions, or large pieces of molecules. Only the physical change occurs in this technique depending on the temperature and atmospheric pressure.
