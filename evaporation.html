Evaporation

One way of separating a solid from a liquid in a mixture is through evaporation. Through the process of evaporation the liquid turns into gas, leaving the solid substance behind.

This diagram shows how Evaporation is used to sediments from water. The water is made to evaporate by using a burner. The water evaporates and turns into gas, leaving the sediments behind. This separates the mixture.
