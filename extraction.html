Extraction

Extraction uses a solvent that is selected to remove or extract one or more substances from the mixture. The mixture is combined with the solvent during the extraction, dissolving the unwanted substance while leaving the desired substance behind.

This diagram shows how Extraction is used in separating mixtures. The solvent extracts the blue particles from the solution, leaving only the pink particles behind. The pink particles are then collected and put into a different container.
