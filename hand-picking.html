Hand-picking

The hand-picking method is a way to separate different components in a combination by simply picking them out of the mixture with your hand. It is one of the many techniques that is used in dry environments. The substances that are separated using this approach may be useful products, contaminants that need to be eliminated, or both of the separated substances may be beneficial products. Just little amounts of the substance that needs to be separated are suitable for this approach. With the hand-picking technique, the materials can be divided according to their size, color, shape, weight, etc.
