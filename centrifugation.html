Centrifugation

Centrifugation is a technique used in chemistry to use centrifugal forces to separate mixtures of different compounds. Centrifugation makes use of centrifugal force to force the materials against the wall of the tube, separating the mixture.

This diagram shows how Centrifugation is used to separate mixtures. Because of the cetrifugal force, the substances that make up the mixture will separate from each other.
