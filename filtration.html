Filtration

Filtration is the process in which a mixture is put through a filter medium that separates the solid particles from the liquid substance. The filter catches the solid particles and lets the liquid pass through, effectively separating them.

This diagram shows how filtration is used to filter out dirty particles from water, separating the unwanted substances from the liquid and leaving clean water.
