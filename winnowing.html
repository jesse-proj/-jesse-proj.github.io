Winnowing

Is a separating technique where heavier components mixtures are separated from lighter components thru the wind. This method is popular in agriculture wherein one of its uses is to separate pests from stored grain by throwing the two mixtures in the air so that the pests will be blown away and the grains fall back.

This diagram shows how Winnowing is used to separate different types of seeds. The light seeds land further away than the heavy seeds.
